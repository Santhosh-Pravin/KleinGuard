# KleinGuard Demo Video Script

This file is a practical script for recording the required 2-minute demo video.

## Goal

Show that KleinGuard protects gig workers through:

- onboarding,
- policy generation,
- ML-based premium pricing,
- automated disruption detection,
- zero-touch claims.

## Suggested 2-Minute Flow

### 0:00 - 0:20

Introduce the problem:

"Gig workers lose income when disruptions like rain, flood, AQI spikes, traffic, or platform demand drops affect their working hours. KleinGuard is a parametric protection system that automatically detects those disruptions and creates income-protection claims with minimal user effort."

### 0:20 - 0:45

Show registration:

- Open the registration flow.
- Enter worker details.
- Verify OTP using `123456`.
- Select city, platform, and work zones.

Narration:

"The worker signs up in under two minutes. We capture platform, city, work hours, and delivery zones so protection can be personalized to the worker’s actual earning context."

### 0:45 - 1:10

Show dynamic premium and policy generation:

- Move to the risk/policy screen.
- Explain the premium factors.
- Highlight ML-based pricing and zone-aware risk.

Narration:

"The weekly premium is calculated dynamically using a machine-learning service that considers flood probability, AQI, demand volatility, trust score, recent claim activity, and selected coverage amount."

### 1:10 - 1:35

Show automated trigger and claim creation:

- Open dashboard/demo controls.
- Fire one or two disruption triggers.
- Show live updates or resulting claims.

Narration:

"When a disruption signal is detected, KleinGuard checks whether the worker is active in the affected zone, estimates lost income, evaluates fraud risk, and creates the claim automatically."

### 1:35 - 1:55

Show claims management:

- Open claims screen.
- Show auto-approved or under-review states.
- Point out payout amount and audit trail.

Narration:

"Low-risk claims can move straight to payout. Higher-risk claims go to review. This creates a zero-touch experience for genuine users while still protecting the system."

### 1:55 - 2:00

Close:

"KleinGuard protects workers before, during, and after disruptions by combining hyper-local automation, machine learning, and seamless claims."

## Recording Tips

- Keep browser zoom readable
- Use the seeded demo account
- Refresh once before recording
- Keep narration simple and product-focused
- Show one trigger clearly instead of many fast clicks
